We Bring Real Time Peace of Mind To Caregivers

The Soteria Solution

We are developing a simple innovation that will help keep patients with Alzheimers, dementia, and similar challenges safe while maintaining their sense of dignity and autonomy. Soteria will combine comfortable and discreet wearables with cutting-edge behaviorial predictive analytics to provide caregiver peace of mind in real time.

Who Are We?

Erika Harvey

After the death of her husband due to a glitch in the hospitals EHR system, Erika left her position as a nurse and nursing instructor and threw herself into the deep end of the tech pool. there she spent a year focusing on learning how to code in three full stacks and found her niche after apprenticing as an UX researcher. Still not satisfied with providing a better world for her patients through tech, she came up with the idea of moxxie.io and Patient Advocacy Through Technology is her personal mantra.

Susan Zell

After completing a Bachelor's degree in Psychology from Swarthmore College and returning to her childhood home in the Chicago suburbs, Susan honed the art of customer service for several years before discovering her true calling as a web developer. She began a rigorous course of self-study, rekindling her dreams of entrepreneurship with a deep dive into both programming and learning how businesses succeed or fail. She finally had the opportunity to move to Silicon Valley where she immediately dove into the world of competitive hackathons. Within months, she had figured out the secrets of idea generation, teambuilding, and pitch-perfect pitches, quickly rattling off three wins in a row. She is very excited about applying her brainpower and enthusiam to this venture.
